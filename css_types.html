Hargeisa

Hargeisa is the capital city of Somaliland, an autonomous region in the Horn of Africa. It's one of the largest cities in Somaliland and serves as its political, economic, and cultural center. Hargeisa has a rich history and is known for its bustling markets, vibrant culture, and significant landmarks like the Independence Monument and the War Memorial.
